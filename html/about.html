About SilkValley

The knit and silk business house of SilkValley is one of the pioneers in establishing garments revolution in Bangladesh. Today it is a role model for the nation in the field of combining various sectors, such us IT, manufacturing, trading and service to attain synergistic growth through expansion and diversification.

The Essence of SilkValley is a statement of our unwavering credo as an organization.

We are well-diversified business house, with clients in over 30 countries, delivering complex and challenging projects for several years, We operate in 10 major business areas with over 15 group companies and a strong employee base of over 500 people from different nationalities. Above all, we are committed to delivering projects and solutions globally with the help of breakthrough technology that will leave an impact for generation to come.

Mission To prosper together with customers, shareholders, and employees, and to contribute to a prosperous society.

Vision To deliver customer satisfaction with creativity, innovation, sincerity, and gratitude, and to act in compliance.
